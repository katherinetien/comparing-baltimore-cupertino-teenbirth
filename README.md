# Comparing Teenage Birth Rate in Baltimore, MD and San Francisco, CA with Opportunity Atlas Data
## Background Information 

I worked as a research intern with the Baltimore City Health Department for a federally-funded project to address health-related social needs of pregnant women last summer. One day at work, as I sat across from the fourth pregnant teenager I had interviewed that week, I knew I had stumbled upon an important (though often overlooked) public health crisis in Baltimore: teen birth.
